FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?+
We recommend 6–12 months for weddings, 2–3 months for corporate events and parties. We also accommodate last-minute bookings subject to availability.
Do you manage destination weddings outside Surat?+
Absolutely! We specialise in Udaipur, Goa, Jaipur, Mumbai, and international destinations including Thailand, Bali, and Dubai.
What is your payment structure?+
3-stage payment: 30% booking advance, 40% thirty days before the event, 30% on event day. We accept all major payment methods.
Can we meet before booking?+
Of course! Free 30-minute consultation at our Surat office or via video call. Helps us understand your vision before committing.
